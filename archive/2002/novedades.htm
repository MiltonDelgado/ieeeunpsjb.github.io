Student Paper Contest

31 de mayo - fecha límite para la entrega de trabajos del Student Paper Contest del 2002 en la R9. Este concurso es auspiciado por el IEEE Internacional y conlleva los siguientes premios para los ganadores: US$800, US$500 y US$200. Las ponencias deben competir primero a nivel de país o Sección y las tres mejores pasarán a la competencia final de la Región.
Los documentos de soporte se pueden consultar en:
http://www.ewh.ieee.org/reg/9/comites/actividades_estudiantiles/index.htm
Para más información acerca de la elaboración de los trabajos, consulte los documentos guiaarticulos.pdf, plantillaarticulo.pdf, y plantillaarticulo.doc en la página de la Región:
http://www.ewh.ieee.org/reg/9/comites/actividades_estudiantiles

IEEE Internacional

IEEE Spectrum Launches Careers Web Site

13 June - http://www.spectrum.ieee.org/careers. The site showcases articles from the magazine's Careers section as well as links to other IEEE career tools and resources. Visitors also may sign up for the new weekly e-mail careers newsletter, IEEE Career Alert. The newsletter will focus on career issues, and include contributions from IEEE Spectrum, other IEEE and non-IEEE sources.

To subscribe, visit http://www.spectrum.ieee.org/careers/signup.html

IEEE Xplore™ 1.4 Adds New Tools For Researchers

19 April - The latest version of the IEEE Xplore™ online delivery platform contains several features designed to help make research faster and more efficient. They include:
- A subscription identifier, a "What can I access?" button, to help users better understand their content access rights.
- Forward linking from abstracts, to show "documents that cite this document."
- Links from author names, to perform an author search within the abstract record.
- Links from index terms, to perform a subject term search within the abstract record.
- Downloadable citations, enabling subscribers to use bibliographic software.

IEEE Xplore™ Tops 750,000 Documents

5 March - On 27 February, the IEEE Xplore™ online delivery system hit a milestone -- 750,000 technical documents. Each week, the system is updated with new papers from conference proceedings and journals, articles from magazines, and standards.

IEEE Announces New Society and Council

5 March - The IEEE has established the IEEE Neural Networks Society and the IEEE Nanotechnology Council, replacing the IEEE Neural Networks Council and the IEEE Nanotechnology Committee, respectively. This brings the total number to 37 IEEE societies and four technical councils.

IEEE Computer Society Offers Distance Learning Courses Free to Members

1 Feb - During 2002, members of the IEEE Computer Society have fee-free access to 100 interactive, Web-based course titles in 17 subject areas. Topics include Java, project management, Cisco, Windows network security, Unix and HTML. Many of the courses are designed to help computing professionals prepare for certification exams. For more information, visit the IEEE Computer Society's Distance Learning Campus.
